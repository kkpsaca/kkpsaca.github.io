About the Firm

Two Decades of Trusted Practice

Established in 2004, P Sreenivasulu and Associates has built a reputation for technical excellence, professional integrity, and client-first service. Our firm holds empanelments with ICAI, RBI, C&AG, and the Karnataka State Cooperative Audit Department — a testament to the trust institutions place in our competence.

B.Com FCA ACS DISA(ICAI)

CA P. Sreenivasulu

Founder Partner · Established 2004

CA P. Sreenivasulu is the Founder Partner of the firm, bringing over two decades of rich experience in Chartered Accountancy practice. His academic background combines a Bachelor of Commerce with the prestigious Fellow Membership of ICAI (FCA) — the highest grade of ICAI membership awarded to practitioners of sustained excellence.

What truly distinguishes him is his rare dual qualification as both a Fellow Chartered Accountant (FCA) and an Associate Company Secretary (ACS) — enabling the firm to offer integrated financial and corporate law services under one roof. His DISA certification from ICAI further enables him to lead information systems audit engagements with specialist competence.

His practice spans statutory and internal audits, income tax planning and compliance, and comprehensive secretarial and compliance advisory for companies of all sizes. He is the trusted advisor to a diverse client base ranging from emerging startups to established enterprises across Karnataka.

CA P. Krishna Kanth

Partner · Technology & Taxation Specialist

CA P. Krishna Kanth is a partner who brings a truly distinctive profile to the firm — a graduate of the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NIT-K, Surathkal), one of India's premier engineering institutions, who later qualified as a Chartered Accountant. This rare combination of engineering rigour and financial expertise creates a professional uniquely equipped for today's technology-driven business environment.

His Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) certification — a globally recognised credential — enables him to conduct IS audits and forensic engagements at the highest level. He combines this with deep expertise in direct and indirect taxation, cyber law advisory, and ERP system design, making him an invaluable resource for technology companies and businesses undergoing digital transformation.

CA Krishna Kanth's practice is particularly valued by technology companies, manufacturing firms, and businesses that require an advisor who can bridge the worlds of finance, technology, and compliance. His forensic audit capabilities and cyber law expertise are sought by organisations navigating digital risks and investigations.
