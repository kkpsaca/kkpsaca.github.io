Our Services

Comprehensive financial, legal, and advisory solutions — delivered with precision and professional integrity.

Service 01

Audit & Assurance

Our audit practice is built on a foundation of technical rigour and independence. Whether it's a statutory requirement, an internal control review, or a specialised IS audit, we bring meticulous attention to every engagement. Our partners hold DISA and CISA qualifications, giving us a unique edge in information systems audit — a capability increasingly critical in today's digital business environment.

Statutory Audits

  • Company audit under Companies Act 2013
  • Bank branch statutory audits
  • Cooperative society audits
  • Trust and NGO audits
  • LLP statutory audits

Internal & Concurrent Audits

  • Internal audit of companies and banks
  • Concurrent audit of bank branches
  • Revenue audit engagements
  • Stock audit and physical verification
  • Process and control reviews

IS & Specialised Audits

  • Information Systems (IS) audits
  • ERP system audits and reviews
  • Forensic audits and investigations
  • C&AG empanelled audit assignments
  • Due diligence audits
Service 02

Taxation Services

Navigating India's complex tax landscape demands both expertise and vigilance. Our taxation practice covers the full spectrum — from individual income tax planning to corporate direct tax compliance, GST advisory, and representation before tax authorities. We combine legal knowledge with practical business understanding to minimise your tax burden within the bounds of law.

Direct Taxation

  • Individual and HUF income tax filing
  • Corporate tax planning and compliance
  • TDS compliance and returns
  • Tax audit under Section 44AB
  • Representation before CIT(A) / ITAT

Indirect Taxation

  • GST registration and returns
  • GST audit and reconciliation
  • Input tax credit optimisation
  • GST notices and assessments
  • Customs and import duty advisory

Advisory & Planning

  • Tax-efficient business structuring
  • Capital gains tax planning
  • International taxation and DTAA
  • Transfer pricing documentation
  • Tax health checks
Service 03

Corporate & Secretarial

Our firm's unique edge lies in the ACS qualification held by our Founder Partner — enabling us to offer integrated corporate law and secretarial services alongside traditional CA services. From incorporation to ongoing compliance, we handle all aspects of your corporate lifecycle, freeing you to focus on business growth.

Company Formation

  • Private / Public company incorporation
  • LLP formation and registration
  • Section 8 (non-profit) company
  • OPC (One Person Company)
  • Partnership firm registration

Ongoing Compliance

  • Annual ROC filings (AOC-4, MGT-7)
  • Board meeting and AGM support
  • Maintenance of statutory registers
  • Director KYC and DIN compliance
  • Share transfer and allotment

Corporate Advisory

  • FEMA and RBI compliances
  • Mergers and amalgamations
  • Strike off / winding up advisory
  • Secretarial audit (Form MR-3)
  • Corporate restructuring advisory

Specialised Services

C&AG Empanelled Services

Cooperative & Cyber
