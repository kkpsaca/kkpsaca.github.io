Our Professional Services

We offer a comprehensive range of chartered accountant services designed to meet all your financial and business needs. Our experienced team provides expert guidance and solutions tailored to your specific requirements across diverse industry sectors.

1. Audit & Assurance

Comprehensive audit and assurance services ensuring transparency, compliance, and stakeholder confidence across all business operations.

Statutory & Compliance Audits

  • Statutory Audits of Companies, LLPs, Firms, Trusts & Societies
  • Tax Audits & GST Audits
  • Special Audits under RBI, C&AG, and Regulatory Authorities

Specialized Audits

  • Internal & Management Audits
  • Concurrent, Revenue & Stock Audits of Banks & Corporates
  • System & Information Systems (IS) Audits

Investigation & Forensic

  • Forensic Audits & Fraud Detection
  • Due Diligence Services
  • Risk Assessment & Management

2. Taxation Services

Complete direct and indirect taxation solutions covering compliance, advisory, and representation services.

Direct Taxation

  • Income Tax Advisory & Compliance
  • Filing of Returns for Corporates, Firms & Individuals
  • Tax Planning & Structuring
  • Representation before Income Tax Authorities (Assessment & Appeals)
  • TDS Compliance & Advisory

Indirect Taxation

  • GST Registration, Compliance & Filing
  • GST Audit & Advisory
  • Representation before GST Authorities
  • Advisory on Input Tax Credit, Refunds & Transitional Provisions

3. Corporate & Secretarial Services

End-to-end corporate compliance and secretarial services ensuring regulatory adherence and smooth business operations.

Business Formation

  • Incorporation of Companies, LLPs & Partnerships
  • Corporate Law & ROC Compliance
  • Maintenance of Statutory Records & Filings

Corporate Restructuring

  • Advisory on Mergers, Restructuring & Conversions
  • Secretarial Compliance for Private & Public Companies
  • Corporate Governance Advisory

Non-Profit Organizations

  • Trusts, Societies & NGO Compliance
  • Regulatory Filings & Approvals
  • Statutory Record Maintenance

4. Business & Management Consultancy

Strategic business advisory services helping organizations optimize operations, manage risks, and achieve sustainable growth.

Business Strategy

  • Business Setup & Structuring Advisory
  • Preparation of Business Plans & Financial Projections
  • Strategic Planning & Market Analysis

Technology & Systems

  • System Design, ERP Consulting & Process Automation
  • Internal Controls & Risk Management
  • Digital Transformation Advisory

Governance & Compliance

  • Advisory on Corporate Governance & Compliance Frameworks
  • Risk Assessment & Mitigation Strategies
  • Performance Management Systems

5. Investment & Financial Advisory

Comprehensive financial advisory services covering investments, valuations, and strategic financial planning for sustainable growth.

Investment Services

  • Advisory on Investments & Wealth Management
  • Portfolio Analysis & Optimization
  • Risk Assessment & Management

Valuation & Due Diligence

  • Business Valuation & Due Diligence
  • Project Financing & Loan Syndication Support
  • Financial Analysis & Feasibility Studies

Strategic Financial Planning

  • Financial Restructuring & Strategic Planning
  • Capital Structure Optimization
  • Merger & Acquisition Advisory

6. Specialized Services

Specialized and sector-specific services leveraging our empanelments and expertise across diverse industries and regulatory frameworks.

Empanelled Services

  • Empanelled Audits with RBI, C&AG & Co-operative Audit Departments
  • Bank Audits & Financial Institution Reviews
  • Government & Public Sector Audits

Startup & NRI Services

  • Assistance in Government Registrations & Approvals
  • Advisory for Non-Resident Indians (NRI) on Tax & Investments

Sector-Specific Advisory
