Life at PS&A

Why Work With Us

We invest in people who want to build a meaningful career in Chartered Accountancy.

Structured Learning

Systematic training across all service lines — audit, taxation, corporate law, and IS auditing — with guided supervision by qualified partners.

Direct Mentorship

Work directly alongside partners with FCA, ACS, DISA, and CISA qualifications. Gain exposure to high-quality, diverse engagements from day one.

Diverse Client Exposure

Engage with clients from banking, technology, manufacturing, cooperatives, government undertakings, and more — building a well-rounded professional profile.

Work-Life Balance

A structured work schedule with clear expectations. We believe sustainable performance requires balance — not relentless overwork.

Ethics & Professionalism

A culture built on professional ethics, ICAI guidelines, and mutual respect. The right foundation for a lifelong career in accountancy.

Technology Forward

Work with modern accounting tools, ERP systems, and IS audit methodologies — skills that enhance your value well beyond the articleship period.

Full-Time Articleship Training

Articleship Position

A structured three-year articleship under ICAI regulations, offering broad exposure to audit, taxation, and corporate compliance assignments under direct partner supervision.

Requirements

  • Cleared IPCC / Intermediate (both groups) from ICAI
  • Strong foundation in Accountancy and Taxation
  • Proficiency in MS Excel and Tally
  • Good communication skills in English and Kannada/Telugu
  • Eagerness to learn across service lines

Full-Time Audit & Assurance

Audit Assistant

Support the partners in audit and assurance engagements — including statutory audits, bank branch audits, concurrent audits, and IS audits. An excellent opportunity for recently qualified CAs or semi-qualified candidates with audit experience.

Requirements

  • CA Inter or CA Final qualified preferred
  • 1–2 years of audit experience is an advantage
  • Familiarity with Companies Act 2013 and audit standards
  • Working knowledge of ERP / accounting software
  • Strong analytical ability and attention to detail
