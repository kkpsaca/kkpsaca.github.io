About the Firm

A Legacy Built on Integrity and Expertise

Founded in 2004, P Sreenivasulu and Associates has grown to become a trusted name in Chartered Accountancy services in Bengaluru.

Our firm brings together rare expertise — combining deep traditional accounting knowledge with modern technical capabilities. With partners holding qualifications spanning FCA, ACS, DISA, and CISA, we are uniquely positioned to serve businesses navigating today's complex regulatory and financial landscape.

From statutory audits for large corporations to personalised tax planning for individuals, we approach every engagement with the same commitment to accuracy, timeliness, and professional ethics that has defined us for over two decades.
