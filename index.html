Welcome to P Sreenivasulu and Associates

Founded in 2004, P. Sreenivasulu & Associates is a trusted Chartered Accountancy firm based in Bengaluru, built on a commitment to professional excellence, integrity, and personalized client service. Over the years, we have grown into a multi-partner practice, delivering a full spectrum of financial, tax, and assurance services to corporates, financial institutions, government bodies, and individuals.

With a team of highly qualified partners and professionals, we bring together decades of combined experience across statutory audits, tax advisory, corporate compliance, management consultancy, and specialized assignments. Our expertise particularly extends to bank audits, internal audits, income & revenue audits, system/IS audits, and stock audits, with empanelments across leading public and private sector banks and organizations.

Professional Excellence & Credentials

Our partners hold advanced qualifications such as FCA, ACS, DISA, and CISA, ensuring a blend of traditional accounting knowledge and modern technological proficiency. The firm is registered with ICAI, RBI, C&AG, and Karnataka State Co-op. Audit Dept., underscoring our credibility and trust within the profession.

Beyond financial reporting and compliance, we work as strategic advisors—helping clients optimize resources, ensure regulatory compliance, and achieve sustainable growth. Our long-standing relationships with businesses in diverse sectors, including telecom, healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, and renewable energy, reflect our ability to adapt to evolving client needs.

Our Philosophy: At P. Sreenivasulu & Associates, we believe in combining professional expertise with a personalized approach—making us not just auditors or consultants, but trusted partners in your financial journey.
